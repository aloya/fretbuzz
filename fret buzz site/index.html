Our Gear

Better sound. Period. Music comes from our instruments, not mashed together in a mix. Each member of Fret Buzz has his own array system. Easy to carry on conversation. 

Why 2 cables? The Godin A6 Ultra hybrid is used by both Paul and Alex.The unique feature is the ability to change from acoustic to electric or a mix on the fly. The evening usually start with some good ole favorites tunes.

Once we go electric, Paul sports a Fender Strat and Alex digs in with his Telecaster Amercan Elite.

Whatever isn’t nailed down is probably going to move. Heath plays an SR505. 

Mike is happy with TD-50KV
